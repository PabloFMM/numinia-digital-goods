# Open Source Philosophy

The metaverse should be built on open standards, not locked behind paywalls.

## File Over App

Numinia follows the **File Over App** philosophy: data lives in open files (JSON, GLB, VRM), not in databases controlled by a single company. The app is a viewer — replaceable, forkable, optional. The files are the source of truth.

## Why CC0

All Numinia-curated assets are CC0 (public domain). You can:

- Use in commercial projects
- Modify and remix freely
- No attribution required
- Train AI models
- Use in games, apps, artwork, metaverse worlds

## Decentralized Storage

Assets are stored across multiple layers for resilience:

1. **Cloudflare R2** — Fast CDN delivery
2. **GitHub** — Open, versionable, forkable
3. **Arweave** — Permanent, pay-once storage
4. **IPFS** — Distributed, censorship-resistant

No single point of failure. If one layer goes down, others serve the content.

## Interoperability

We use open formats that work everywhere:

- **GLB/glTF** — Khronos Group standard (Blender, Unity, Unreal, Three.js)
- **VRM** — Open avatar standard (VRChat, Hyperfy, Cluster, many more)
- **HYP** — Hyperfy v2 app format (interactive 3D worlds)

## Built by Numen Games

Numinia Digital Goods is a project by [Numen Games](https://numen.games), building open tools for the 3D internet.

### The Bigger Picture

Numen Games is not just a studio — it's an organizational experiment. Founded in 2020 by five friends and collaborators (the Oracles), Numen Games builds gamified frameworks that transform how organizations work. The core belief: **play is not entertainment, it's how humans learn, grow, and build better relationships.**

Numinia is the narrative projection of this vision — a city-state that exists in the metaverse as a role-playing universe. The digital goods platform is one concrete expression of that universe: a library of free, open assets for anyone building in the 3D internet.

### Three Pillars

- **Craft** — Art is a driver, not decoration. Every creation should carry meaning.
- **Learn** — Humans play to learn. Games are the most natural path to understanding.
- **Remix** — Feel free to copy it and make it better. CC0 is our license because knowledge belongs to everyone.

### The Cause: Digital Humanism

In the transition from the industrial age to the digital age, organizations need new models — more balanced, closer to the human being. Numen Games believes that capitalism can have heart, that technology is a means (not an end), and that the future belongs to those who dare to imagine and play.

### The Relationship Between Game and Platform

The Numinia RPG (a tabletop role-playing game) and Numinia Digital Goods (this platform) share the same universe, values, and philosophy. The game provides the narrative depth — the lore, the guilds, the factions, the mythology. The platform provides the practical infrastructure — open assets that anyone can use to build within that universe or any other.

Together they form what the project calls the **Operating System → Functional Model → Narrative Projection** chain: a real organizational need (Operating System) is given structure (Functional Model) and brought to life through story and play (Narrative Projection).
